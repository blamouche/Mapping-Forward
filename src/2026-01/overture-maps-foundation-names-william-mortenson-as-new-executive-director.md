# Overture Maps Foundation Names William Mortenson as New Executive Director

**Source**: https://overturemaps.org/announcements/2025/overture-maps-foundation-names-william-mortenson-as-new-executive-director/

**Date**: November 3, 2025

**Author**: Overture Maps Foundation

**Keywords**: Overture Maps, Executive Director, Leadership, Open Maps, William Mortenson, Linux Foundation

## Elevator pitch

The Overture Maps Foundation has appointed William Mortenson as its new Executive Director, signaling a strategic commitment to strengthening leadership as the open mapping initiative continues to grow and expand its collaborative ecosystem.

## Takeaways

- William Mortenson has been named the new Executive Director of the Overture Maps Foundation
- The appointment reflects the organization's maturation as a significant player in the open mapping space
- Overture Maps Foundation operates under the Linux Foundation umbrella, bringing together major tech companies
- Strong leadership is critical as the foundation works to create interoperable, open map data
- The announcement demonstrates the foundation's commitment to transparent governance and community engagement

## Synthesis

The Overture Maps Foundation has announced the appointment of William Mortenson as its new Executive Director, marking an important milestone in the organization's evolution. This leadership transition comes at a pivotal time for the foundation, which has been working to establish itself as a major force in the open mapping ecosystem.

The Overture Maps Foundation was established to create reliable, interoperable open map data through collaboration between major technology companies. The consortium includes significant players like Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, and TomTom, all working together to build a comprehensive, openly-licensed map dataset that can be used across different platforms and applications.

The appointment of a dedicated Executive Director signals the foundation's growth and the increasing importance of its mission in the broader technology landscape. Having strong executive leadership is essential for coordinating efforts among the various member organizations, setting strategic priorities, and ensuring the foundation delivers on its promise of creating high-quality, accessible map data.

William Mortenson's selection for this role suggests the foundation is looking for someone who can navigate the complex dynamics of multi-stakeholder collaboration while driving technical innovation. The Executive Director position requires balancing the interests of major corporate members with the broader open-source community's needs, ensuring that the foundation remains true to its mission of openness and interoperability.

The Overture Maps Foundation operates under the Linux Foundation umbrella, which provides governance structure and operational support. This affiliation gives Overture access to proven models for managing large-scale collaborative projects and ensures that the foundation benefits from best practices in open-source project management.

As mapping data becomes increasingly critical for applications ranging from autonomous vehicles to logistics optimization, the need for reliable, standardized map data has never been greater. The Overture Maps Foundation is positioned to address this need by creating a shared data resource that reduces duplication of effort and enables innovation across the industry.

This leadership announcement demonstrates the foundation's commitment to building a sustainable organization capable of delivering long-term value to its members and the broader community. With dedicated executive leadership in place, the Overture Maps Foundation is better positioned to accelerate its roadmap and expand its impact in the years ahead.
